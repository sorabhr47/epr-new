Does EPR Apply to Me?

Yes, if you are any of the following:

Producer
Involved in manufacturing of plastic packaging (plastic sheets, containers, bags, bottles, pellets, etc.).
Brand Owner
Selling commodities under a registered brand label, including online platforms/marketplaces, using plastic packaging to wrap and pack the products.
Importer
Importer of plastic packaging or products wrapped in plastic packaging.
PWP (Plastic Waste Processor)
Recyclers/entities engaged in processing plastic packaging for end-of-life disposal or industrial composting.

Applicable Rules/Acts/Regulations for EPR

1: The Environment (Protection) Act, 1986

The umbrella legislation that empowers the central government to take measures to protect and improve the quality of the environment and prevent, control, and abate environmental pollution. It also provides for the establishment of the National Environmental Appellate Authority, which hears appeals against the orders of the central government or any authority under the act.

2: The E-Waste (Management) Rules, 2016

These rules regulate the generation, collection, storage, transportation, segregation, refurbishment, dismantling, recycling, and disposal of e-waste in India. They define the roles and responsibilities of various stakeholders, specify EPR targets, and outline the procedure for EPR authorization and monitoring.

3: The Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016

These rules regulate the generation, collection, storage, transportation, segregation, processing, and disposal of plastic waste in India. They define the roles of stakeholders and specify EPR targets, registration criteria, and reporting requirements.

4: Guidelines for Implementation of E-Waste (Management) Rules, 2016

Issued by the CPCB to provide clarity and guidance on the implementation of the E-Waste (Management) Rules. They cover aspects such as collection mechanisms, record-keeping, and grievance redressal.

5: Guidelines on Extended Producer Responsibility for Plastic Packaging

Issued by the CPCB to provide clarity and guidance on the Plastic Waste Management Rules. They cover aspects like EPR planning, reporting, verification, and eco-fee mechanisms.
